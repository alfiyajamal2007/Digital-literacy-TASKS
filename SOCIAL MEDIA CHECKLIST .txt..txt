DO’s and DONT’s checklist 


The Do’s:


* Do set your personal accounts to private to control who sees your photos and posts.
* Do use a professional-looking headshot for platforms like LinkedIn.
* Do think twice before posting; if you wouldn't say it in a job interview, don't post it.
* Do engage with your university and professional organizations to build a positive network.
* Do verify the source of news or information before you share it on your feed.


The Don’ts:


* Don't post content that shows illegal activities or violates school policies.
* Don't use social media to badmouth professors, classmates, or your current employer.
* Don't share your private contact information, like your phone number or home address.
* Don't engage in heated arguments or "trolling" that could damage your reputation.
* Don't assume "deleted" posts are gone forever; assume everything is permanent.
